Three most important criteria:

Reliability and Durability:
The reliability of a car is crucial. Consumers often seek vehicles that require minimal repairs, are durable over time, and have a good reputation for mechanical performance.
Fuel Efficiency:
Nowadays,we have to look for cars that offer good fuel economy or use alternative propulsion technologies to reduce fuel consumption and emissions.
Safety:
Safety is a critical criterion when choosing a car. Buyers are interested in safety features such as driver-assistance systems, emergency braking, airbags, advanced safety structures, etc.
